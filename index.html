Cold War

United States VS Soviet Union

What was the cold war?

The Cold War was a major event between the two world superpowers (United States and the Soviet Union), and it lasted from 1947-1991. It was happening all across the globe and has majorly impacted the world today.

The beginning

It began in 1945, World War 2 had just ended and the Allies were deciding what to do with Axis-occupied territory. They decided to divide Europe so Western Europe had capitalist states, and Eastern Europe had communist states. While Germany was divided between a French part, Soviet part, British part, and an American part.
